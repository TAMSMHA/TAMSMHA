Our Mission and Vision

What drives us and where we aim to go.

Mission

To create a vibrant, inclusive, and engaging community within McConnell Hall by organizing events, providing support, and fostering student involvement.

Vision

To be a leading student association that enriches the TAMS experience by promoting personal growth, leadership, and a sense of belonging.

Frequently Asked Questions

McConnell Hall Association (MHA) is a student-led organization dedicated to creating a vibrant and inclusive community through events and activities.

Students can join or participate in MHA events by attending the events, volunteering, or signing up through our website or contact points.
